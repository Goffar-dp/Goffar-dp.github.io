Hey, thanks for stopping by! 👋

I am an MSc (Thesis) student in Statistics at Khulna University, supervised by Dr. Md. Maniruzzaman. My current research (thesis + lab work) focuses on designing an integrated ML + NLP + XAI system for simultaneous detection of depression and anxiety among school teachers in Bangladesh – early results just accepted as a contributory poster at ICASDS 2025

Previously, co-authored a PLOS One article (2025) on trends and determinants of antenatal care use and quality in Bangladesh, and completed my BSc thesis on Fake news detection with XAI – supervised by Sutapa Dey Barna.

In my free time, I enjoy sports, mostly football, travelling, and sleeping.

ragBE – Bilingual Retrieval-Augmented Generation System

A fully functional RAG system for conversational Q&A in English and Bengali. Supports PDF/DOCX/TXT ingestion, multilingual semantic search (paraphrase-multilingual-MiniLM-L12-v2 embeddings), ChromaDB vector store, and Google Gemini LLM orchestration via LangChain.
